  • Malware

    Malware is a term short for malicious software, and is broadly used to refer to any software that gets installed on your digital device and performs unwanted tasks, often for a third party’s benefits. Malware is defined by its malicious intent: acting outside the requirements of the user of a computer.

  • Computer Security

    Computer Security/cyber security is the protection of computing systems and the data that they store or access. Form a general perspective it may look as something really complicated, how else did the wiki leaks happen? But if you narrow it down, it all begins with you, the user.

  • Cryptography

    The word “cryptography” is from Greek words kryptós (hidden) and gráphein (writing). It is a study of mathematical techniques for secure communication, which is using “hidden writing” to keep information secret.

  • Cryptanalysis

    This field is the rival of Cryptography. It focuses on analysing and ultimately breaking a type of code known as ciphers to have access to secret information. This requires a lot of logic and super-difficult mathematical formulae.
